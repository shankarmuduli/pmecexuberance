Parala Maharaja Engineering College

ABOUT PMEC

Parala Maharaja Engineering College (P.M.E.C), Berhampur came into existence in the year 2009 as a constituent college of Biju Pattnaik University of Technology (BPUT),Odisha. This college is one of the premier technical institute in the state and is funded by the government of Odisha.

ABOUT EXEBURANCE

PMEC conducts an annual fest for three days. It is an event packed competitions, performances, star nights, DJ night, Ollywood night and lots more. The competition generally consists of tech fests, race of robotics and cultural talent hunts. Events such as ‘Voice of PMEC’ and ‘Move It’ are a huge success. The star night invites famous Bollywood and Ollywood singers to perform, who rock the stage. DJ night is the only event that can make any person to dance on the beats. Ramp walk excavates the fashion trends of the students. Literatures always create history and competitions brush up the inner talents of the students. Drama has been the greatest source of attraction for many people as we Indian people are very much fond of it. Fests in PMEC are not only celebratory but also educative experience amalgamating fun with culture.
